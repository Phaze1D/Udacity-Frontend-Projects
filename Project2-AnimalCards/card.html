Gray Wolf

The gray wolf has very dense and fluffy winter fur, with short underfur and long, coarse guard hairs.

  • Scientific name: Canis lupus
  • Height: 80 – 85 cm (Adult, At Shoulder)
  • Mass: 30 – 80 kg (Adult)
  • Conservation Status: Population stable

The gray wolf or grey wolf, also known as the timber wolf or western wolf, is a canine native to the wilderness and remote areas of Eurasia and North America.
